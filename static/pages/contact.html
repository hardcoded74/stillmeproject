Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Still Me Project?

The Still Me Project is a nonprofit organization dedicated to preserving identity and dignity for individuals and families navigating memory loss. We create resources, support programs, and community connections that honor the whole person.

Who does your work serve?

We serve individuals experiencing memory loss, their families, and their caregivers. Our resources and programs are designed to support everyone touched by this journey — because memory loss affects entire families and communities, not just one person.

How can I get involved?

There are many ways to support our mission. You can make a financial gift, volunteer your time and expertise, share our work with others, or simply reach out to learn more. Visit our Support page for details.

Where are you located?

Our offices are in Portland, Oregon, but our work reaches families across the country. Many of our resources are available online, and we are always expanding to serve more communities.
