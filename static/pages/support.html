Why It Matters

Your generosity makes this work possible

Behind every resource we create, every family we support, and every story we help preserve, there are people like you who believe that identity and dignity are worth protecting. Your support allows us to reach more families, develop meaningful tools, and build a community where no one navigating memory loss has to feel alone.

Every contribution — no matter the size — has a direct impact. It funds the creation of memory books, family guides, and connection programs. It supports outreach to communities that need us most. And it sends a powerful message to every individual and family we serve: you still matter, and we are here.

Your Impact

Where Your Support Goes

We are deeply intentional about how we use every dollar. Your support directly funds the programs and resources that make our mission real.

  • Family support programs — Guides, workshops, and one-on-one support for families navigating memory loss.
  • Identity preservation resources — Memory books, personal reflections, and tools that hold a person’s story over time.
  • Community education and outreach — Programs that bring awareness, empathy, and practical knowledge to communities.
  • Research and development — Ongoing work to improve our frameworks and create new resources.

When you support this work, you’re telling someone: you still matter.
